### Guide to Filling Out the Variables

Here is a breakdown of what to put in the `<TAGS>` to get the best results:

* `<LIBRARY_PREFERENCE>`: Usually **standard `tkinter**`, **`customtkinter`** (for modern, rounded UIs), or **`ttkbootstrap`** (for Bootstrap-like themes).
* `<GEOMETRY_MANAGER>`:
* **`.grid()`**: Best for complex forms (rows and columns).
* **`.pack()`**: Best for simple vertical/horizontal stacks.
* **`.place()`**: Best for absolute positioning (rarely recommended).


* `<IS_RESIZABLE>`: Usually "True" or "False".
* `<VALIDATION_RULE>`: E.g., "Only integers allowed," "Must be a valid email," or "Cannot be empty."
* `<ACTION_DESCRIPTION>`: Be specific. E.g., "Calculate the tax rate and update the label" or "Save the text content to a .txt file."
